Pizza



Nice bread covered with tomato sauce under a layer of melted mozzarella chesse. MUAH!



Really easy recipe to make, but delicious. A classic in the Argentinas cousin.

(in this recipe we are going to use an store brand ready to use tomato sauce).

Ingredients:

  • Flour 250g
  • Water 125ml
  • Sunflower oil 2 spoons
  • Yeast 1 spoon
  • Sugar 1 spoon
  • Salt 2 spoons
  • Store ready to use tomato sauce.
  • mozzarella chesse.

  1. Put the flour in a bowl and make a volcano form.
  2. Put the yeast and the sugar in the center with 25ml of water and wait for 10 minutes.
  3. Put the oil and the salt at the sides of the volcano.
  4. Put the rest of the water and start kneading the dough.
  5. Once the dough is uniform, let it rest covered for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Stretch it on a pizza font previously oiled.
  7. Put some tomato sauce on top of the uncooked dough and spread it with a spoon.
  8. Put the pizza font on a preheated oven at maxium ultil the dough starts to brown.
  9. Put mozzarella well spread over the pizza and send it back to the oven 5 more minutes.
  10. Take out and enjoy.
