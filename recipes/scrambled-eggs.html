Scrambled eggs



Classic, delicious and simple scramble eggs!



In search of tender, fluffy scrambled eggs? Look no more, you found the recipe you needed.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 teaspoons butter

  1. Whisk eggs with salt until completely combined and very foamy.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over low heat until bubbling. Add whisked eggs; cook, stirring frequently for even, moist curds, until thickened and creamy, but still shiny, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Remove scrambled eggs from the pan before they begin to lok dry, as the will continue to thicken off the heat.
